Why Trempiada?

🚗

Route-matched rides

Drivers set their route and time. Riders who fit the path get matched automatically — no detour, no hassle.

🛡️

Trust & safety

Drivers verify via phone, photo, and social accounts. No numbers shared, chat stays in the bot, plates masked until confirmed.

⭐

Points economy

Rides are free. Drivers earn points for good ratings, redeemable with partner businesses. Good actors get rewarded.
