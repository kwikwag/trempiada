1. Who We Are

Trempiada is a ridesharing service operated via the Telegram messaging platform. It connects drivers who have spare seats with hitchhikers travelling in the same direction, at no charge. The service is delivered entirely through the Telegram bot @TrempiadaBot.

The service is operated by Trempiada Maintainers, Tel Aviv, Israel ("we", "us", or "our"). You can reach us at trempiada-support@googlegroups.com.

This policy explains what personal data we collect, why we collect it, how we protect it, and what rights you have under Israeli law.

2. Scope and Legal Basis

Geographic scope: Trempiada is intended solely for persons who reside in, or are physically located in, Israel. By registering or using the service you confirm that this applies to you. If it does not, you must not use the service. We do not knowingly collect personal data from persons outside Israel.

This policy governs the data we collect and process ourselves. It does not govern data that Telegram processes as part of its own service — for that, please see the Telegram Privacy Policy.

We process your personal data on the following legal grounds:

  • Performance of a service: data necessary to match drivers with riders, manage ride lifecycles, and operate the points economy.
  • Legitimate interests: safety, anti-abuse, ratings integrity, and dispute resolution.
  • Consent: optional verifications (social accounts, email) and optional profile photo.

The collection and processing of personal data is governed by the Israeli Privacy Protection Law, 5741–1981, as comprehensively amended by Amendment 13 (August 2024), and by the Privacy Protection Regulations (Information Security), 5777–2017.

3. Data We Collect

We only collect data that is strictly necessary for the service to function. All data is stored in a local database operated by us; it is never sold.

3.2 Identity verifications (drivers & riders)

To use the service as a driver you must complete at least one verification. Each verification record stores:

  • Verification type: phone, photo, car, facebook, linkedin, google, or email
  • Whether you have chosen to make it visible to riders (you control this at any time via /profile)
  • An external reference where applicable — for example, a social profile URL or email address, used only to confirm the verification and never displayed to other users
  • Timestamp of verification

3.3 Car registration (drivers only)

  • Licence plate number (partially masked to riders until a match is confirmed)
  • Make, model, colour, year
  • Number of seats
  • Car photo — stored as a Telegram file ID; the image is never downloaded or stored by us locally
Car photo & AI processing: When you submit a car photo during registration, the image is downloaded from Telegram and passed to the Google Gemini Vision API to automatically extract the licence plate number and car details. The image is transmitted to Google's servers for this single purpose and is subject to Google's Privacy Policy. We do not retain the image after processing.

3.4 Ride offers (drivers)

  • Origin and destination coordinates (latitude/longitude) and human-readable labels
  • Planned route geometry (an encoded path on Israeli road network; computed by our self-hosted routing server)
  • Estimated route duration
  • Departure time
  • Maximum acceptable detour (minutes)
  • Available seats
  • Ride status and timestamps

3.5 Ride requests (riders)

  • Pickup and dropoff coordinates and labels
  • Earliest and latest acceptable departure time
  • Status and creation timestamp

3.6 Matches

When a driver and rider are paired, we record:

  • References to the ride offer and ride request
  • Agreed pickup and dropoff coordinates
  • Added detour duration
  • A 4-digit pickup confirmation code used at the roadside to verify identities; it is not used once the ride has begun
  • Match status and lifecycle timestamps (accepted, picked up, completed)
  • Points transferred
  • Cancellation reason and which party cancelled, if applicable

3.7 Ratings

After each completed ride, both parties rate each other simultaneously (1–5 stars) and may leave an optional text comment. We store the scores, comments, and timestamps. Ratings are revealed to both parties at the same time to prevent retaliatory rating.

3.8 Disputes

If you report an issue with a completed match via /sos or the dispute flow, we store your user ID, the match reference, your free-text description, and any resolution notes.

5. Data Shared Between Users

We share the minimum information necessary to make a ride work safely. No Telegram usernames, phone numbers, or direct contact details are ever shared between users by the service.

What riders see about a driver (before confirming a match)

  • First name
  • Gender
  • Trust verification badges — only those the driver explicitly chose to share
  • Average driver rating and number of rides completed
  • Car make, model, colour, year, and seat count
  • Licence plate — partially masked (e.g. "12-??-456") until both parties confirm the match

What drivers see about a rider (before confirming a match)

  • First name
  • Trust verification badges the rider chose to share
  • Average rider rating

After match confirmation

  • The full licence plate is revealed to the rider
  • A 4-digit confirmation code is provided to both parties for use at pickup

6. Third-Party Services

Telegram

All interaction with Trempiada flows through the Telegram platform. Telegram receives messages you send and delivers responses. Telegram's own data practices are governed by the Telegram Privacy Policy. Trempiada operates in compliance with the Telegram Standard Bot Privacy Policy and the Telegram Bot Developer Terms.

Google (Gemini Vision API)

Car photos submitted during driver registration are downloaded from Telegram and passed once to the Google Gemini Vision API to extract vehicle details (licence plate, make, model, colour, year). This transfer occurs only at registration time and the image is not retained by us after processing. Google's handling of this data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

OSRM routing server (self-hosted)

Route and detour calculations are performed on a self-hosted OSRM instance using Israeli OpenStreetMap data. Coordinates are sent only to our own server; no location data is transmitted to any external routing service.

No other third parties

We do not use analytics platforms, advertising networks, or any other third-party data processors beyond those listed above.

7. In-Ride Message Relay

During an active match, non-command messages you send to the bot are forwarded to the other party in real time. These relay messages are not stored in our database — they pass through the bot transiently and are not retained after delivery. This relay mechanism exists so that drivers and riders can communicate without exchanging personal contact details.

8. Data Security

We classify our information systems under the Privacy Protection Regulations (Information Security), 5777–2017 at the medium security level (רמת אבטחה בינונית), which applies given the nature of data we process (location data, phone numbers, and photographs). In accordance with that classification we maintain the following measures:

  • Access controls: access to personal data is restricted to authorised personnel with defined, role-based permissions. No personal data is exposed through a public-facing interface.
  • Physical and environmental security: systems holding personal data are operated on secured infrastructure with physical access restrictions.
  • Data minimisation in transit: licence plates are partially masked when shown to riders until a match is mutually confirmed; no personal contact details are shared between users by the service.
  • Image handling: profile and car photos are not stored by us — only the reference identifiers issued by Telegram are retained.
  • Security incident procedures: we maintain procedures for detecting, containing, and reporting security incidents.
  • Periodic review: security measures are reviewed periodically in accordance with Regulation 16.

In the event of a personal data breach likely to affect your rights, we will notify you and, where required, the Israeli Privacy Protection Authority, as mandated by Amendment 13 to the Privacy Protection Law.

9. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active and for a reasonable period thereafter to allow for dispute resolution and legal compliance. The following principles apply:

  • Active account data (profile, verifications, car) — retained while your account is active.
  • Ride and match records — retained for 2 years after the ride date for dispute resolution and regulatory purposes.
  • Ratings and comments — retained as part of the ride record.
  • Dispute records — retained for 5 years after resolution.

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time — see Section 10. We will honour deletion requests within 30 days, subject to any mandatory retention obligations under applicable law.

You can delete your account at any time by sending /delete to @TrempiadaBot and confirming. Deletion is permanent and immediate.

10. Your Rights

Under the Israeli Privacy Protection Law (as amended by Amendment 13, 2024) you have the right to:

  • Access: request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: ask us to correct inaccurate data.
  • Deletion: ask us to delete your data, subject to legal retention requirements.
  • Restriction / objection: ask us to restrict or stop processing your data in certain circumstances.
  • Withdrawal of consent: where processing is based on consent (e.g. optional verifications), withdraw that consent at any time by sending a message to the bot or contacting us.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address in Section 13. We will respond within 30 days.

If you believe your privacy rights have been violated, you may lodge a complaint with the Israeli Privacy Protection Authority (PPA): www.gov.il — Privacy Protection Authority.

11. Children

The service is intended for users aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has registered, please contact us and we will promptly delete the account.
